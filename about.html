Our Team

Joe Perry

Company Director

Specialising in asset verification services for the facilities management industry, bringing over 15 years of hands-on and leadership experience across complex commercial environments.

Originally qualifying as an HVAC engineer, I began my career as a mobile engineer before progressing through supervisory and management roles. This included serving as Site Supervisor on a large, high-end estate, where I was responsible for operational delivery, compliance, and asset performance across critical systems.

I later advanced into a Contract Manager role, overseeing diverse portfolios and multidisciplinary teams while maintaining a strong focus on accuracy, risk reduction, and lifecycle asset management. This progression has given me a deep understanding of FM operations from both a technical and commercial perspective.

My background enables me to bridge the gap between engineering, compliance, and management by delivering precise, reliable asset verification that supports informed decision-making, regulatory compliance, and long-term asset value for FM clients.
