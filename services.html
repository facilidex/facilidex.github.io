Services

Our services deliver accurate asset verification, condition reporting, and site issue assessments to support efficient facility management and cost planning. Each service provides your business with clear, structured data to simplify mobilisation, maintenance decisions, and future works forecasting.

  • Asset Verification
  • MEP Condition Reporting
  • Issue Reporting
  • Facility Management Consultancy

Asset Verification

Comprehensive asset verification delivered in Excel format to support seamless mobilisation and CAFM system uploads. Each survey captures detailed, structured data to ensure accuracy, compliance, and efficient lifecycle management, including:

  • Make, model and serial numbers
  • Data plate photography
  • Precise asset location
  • Access considerations (including access panels, high-level access, and accurate floor-to-ceiling heights)
  • Identification of critical plants
  • Specialist requirements per asset to support subcontractor costing
  • Parent/child asset relationships for linked systems (e.g. split systems and VRVs)
  • Out-of-hours access requirements
  • SFG20 maintenance codes (where access to your organisation's SFG20 licence is available)

MEP Condition Reporting

Structured MEP condition reporting designed to support informed budgeting and service cost planning. Reports utilise a clear RAG rating system, with optional budget costings where required:

  • Red – Immediate remedial works required
  • Amber – Works anticipated within 1–3 years
  • Green – No significant works expected for 4+ years

This approach enables clear prioritisation of works and transparent financial forecasting.

Additional Services

On-site walk-through inspections to identify potential issues relating to Fire Risk Assessments (FRAs), Legionella Risk Assessments (LRAs), and general building works.

A concise issues report is provided to highlight areas of concern and create a clear pathway for recommended and quoted remedial works.
