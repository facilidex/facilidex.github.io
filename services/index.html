Services

Our services deliver accurate asset verification, condition reporting, and site issue assessments to support efficient facility management and cost planning. Each service provides your business with clear, structured data to simplify mobilisation, maintenance decisions, and future works forecasting.

  • On-Site Auditing
  • Asset Verification
  • MEP Condition Reporting
  • Issue Reporting

Asset Verification

Detailed asset verification sent in excel format for seamless mobilisation and CAFM upload, capturing information including:

  • Make/model/serial numbers
  • Data plate photos
  • Location
  • Limited access, such as access panels and high level including accurrate floor to ceiling height
  • Critical plant
  • Specialists required per asset for easy subcontractor costing
  • Parent/child assets for linked systems such as splits and VRVs
  • Out of hours
  • SFG20 codes (subject to access to company SFG20)

MEP Condition Reporting

Condition report to aid with budgeting and service costs:

  • RAG style reporting of plant condition with optional budgets
  • Red: immediate remedial works required
  • Amber: works expected within 1-2 years
  • Green: no considerable works expected for 4+ years

Other Services

Issues report created on site walk around to highlight potential problems relating to FRAs, LRAs and general works. This simple report will demonstrate an open mind to your customer and create an easy in road to quoted works.
